My Town

I live in Lexington ma, and in the center of my town is a battle green where the opening shots of the american revolutionary war were shot. As a symbol of this a statue of paul revere is placed at this battle green.

In my town my favorite places to eat is Love at First Bite, a Thai restaurant and Beijing, a Chinese restaurant.

In my town we have public library and a public pool that is open in the summer.

In the lexington center there are a few good bakerys such as When Pigs Fly and Bread Obsession.
